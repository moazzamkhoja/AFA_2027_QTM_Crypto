### NVT/G — Levelized PQ approach
- Project TXN forward 10 years at trailing 3-year CAGR
- Terminal value at g_term = 3%, discounted at CAPM r_e
- PQ* = PV of projected TXN stream (levelized)
- NVT_fundamental = MC / PQ*
- Simple version (MC / current TXN × growth rate) tested as robustness

### Asset Universe
- **Coins**: L1 PoS chains with native staking (ETH, SOL, AVAX, BNB, ATOM, ADA, etc.)
  - Exclude L2s (security derived from ETH, not independent monetary coordination)
- **Tokens**: DeFi governance tokens (UNI, AAVE, COMP, CRV, GMX, LDO, MKR, etc.)
  - Include regardless of chain
- Survivorship bias: include delisted assets from CoinGecko/DeFiLlama
- Minimum threshold: ≥6 months data, ≥$10M average market cap

### Cross-Sectional Classification
- Coins and tokens analyzed **separately**
- High/low defined by **within-group median** at each rebalancing date (monthly)
- Token protocol-type fixed effects as control (DEX, lending, liquid staking, infrastructure)
- Within-group tests as supplemental robustness

### Portfolio Construction
- **Primary**: Long Stars / Short Avoids, equal-weighted, monthly rebalancing
- **Factor model**: regress on 3 factors — market (BTC), size (log MC), momentum (3-month)
- **Alpha** = unexplained return after factor adjustment
- **Sharpe ratios** reported for Stars-only, Avoids-only, long-short, benchmark
- Separate results for coins sample and tokens sample
